📊 Global E-Commerce Sales Power BI Dashboard

A Business Intelligence project developed using **Microsoft Power BI** to analyze and visualize global e-commerce sales data. This dashboard transforms raw business data into meaningful insights through interactive reports, helping users understand sales performance, customer behavior, product trends, and overall business growth.

---

📖 Project Overview

The **Global E-Commerce Sales Power BI Dashboard** was developed as part of my internship at **IZEON Innovative Private Limited**. The main objective of this project was to apply Business Intelligence concepts to real-world business data and create an interactive dashboard that supports data-driven decision-making.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of key business metrics such as total sales, profit, customer satisfaction, regional performance, payment methods, product categories, and sales channels. It demonstrates how Microsoft Power BI can transform complex datasets into clear, interactive, and meaningful visualizations.

📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard includes the following business metrics:

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Total Orders
* Customer Satisfaction
* Average Delivery Time

These KPIs provide a quick overview of overall business performance and help users monitor important trends.

---

📈 Dashboard Insights

The dashboard helps answer important business questions, including:

* Which region generates the highest sales?
* Which product categories perform the best?
* How does customer satisfaction vary across regions?
* Which payment methods are most commonly used?
* Which sales channels contribute the most revenue?
* How have sales changed over time?

These insights enable businesses to make informed decisions and improve operational performance.
